Amelia (Meels) Fink

Author | Artist | Speaker

Amelia Fink (aka Meels!!) is an aspiring writer currently purusuing her passion at RMIT Melbourne through a Bachelors in Creative Writing. She is currently writing an novel which has been in the works for around 3 or 4 years! (There has been many scrapped drafts and much procrastination (ᴗ˳ᴗ)ᶻ𝗓𐰁 .ᐟ)

She mainly writes fiction, gearing towards the speculative-fiction, dystopian, and magical realism genres. Through character-driven narratives and immersive world-building, she explores themes of humanity, love, and the ugly, complex, beautiful human experience.

Besides writing, Meels is passionate about game design and storytelling, coding, animation, and her friends! xx
